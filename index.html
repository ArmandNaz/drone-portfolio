Armand Nazarians is a Los Angeles-based aerial cinematographer specializing in real estate, community, and landscape drone work across Southern California.

With a meticulous eye for composition and light, each flight is planned to capture the character of a place — not just its geometry. From the gated estates of Porter Ranch to sprawling civic parks nestled against the Santa Susana Mountains, the work balances technical precision with genuine artistry.

Available for real estate listings, developer showcases, community profiles, and commercial productions throughout the greater Los Angeles area.
